Privacy Policy for Juice Cost

Last updated: March 2026

Juice Cost stores app settings, charging history, and related app data locally on the user’s device. If iCloud sync is enabled, this data may also sync to the user’s personal iCloud account using Apple iCloud services.

Juice Cost does not use its own external servers to store user charging data. Synced data is stored through Apple’s iCloud services in the user’s own iCloud account.

Juice Cost does not sell personal information, does not use data for advertising, and does not track users across apps or websites.

If analytics, third-party services, login features, or additional online functionality are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated.
