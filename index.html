Privacy Policy for Juice Cost

Last updated: December 2025

Juice Cost does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The app does not require account creation and does not transmit user-entered charging settings, charging history, or calculation results to external servers.

Any settings, preferences, or saved charging history are stored locally on the user’s device.

Juice Cost does not sell personal information and does not use personal data for tracking or advertising.

If analytics, cloud sync, login, or other online services are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated.
