Privacy Policy for Juice Cost

Last updated: March 2026

Juice Cost does not collect, transmit, or share personal data.

App settings, charging history, and related data are stored locally on the user’s device using on-device storage. This data is not synced to iCloud and is not stored on external servers controlled by the developer.

Juice Cost does not require account creation, does not use user data for advertising, and does not track users across apps or websites.

If analytics, cloud sync, login features, or other online services are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated.
